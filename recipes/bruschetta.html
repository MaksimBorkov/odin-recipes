Bruschetta al pomodoro

Description

Anything can serve as a filling for bruschetta â€” toasted slices of bread, but the classic version is prepared with tomatoes.

Ingridients

  • 8 slices of bread (ideally homemade bread, such as ciabatta)
  • 1 small red onion (optional)
  • 4 plum-shaped tomatoes (you can take any; the main thing is that they have a dense pulp)
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • a little olive oil
  • basil leaves and dried oregano optional

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes into cubes, removing the seeds, add finely chopped onion to them, add salt and mix.
  2. Fry the bread on the grill, in the oven or toaster until crisp.
  3. Rub the garlic slices a little, sprinkle with olive oil and put the chopped tomatoes.
  4. Add the torn basil leaves and lightly sprinkle with oregano (you can use a ready-made "bouquet" of Italian herbs).
  5. Serve immediately so that the bread does not have time to get wet and become soft.
