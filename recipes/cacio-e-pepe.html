Cacio e pepe

Description

One of the most famous Italian dishes will be mastered even by a novice cook, because only 4 ingredients are required for its preparation.

Ingridients

  • 1 pack of spaghetti
  • 120 g of pecorino romano cheese (if desired, you can replace it with any hard cheese)
  • black pepper-peas (fry it in a frying pan and crush it as you like)
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti to the state of aldente, and while they are cooking, grate the cheese on a fine grater.
  2. In the plate in which you will serve the dish, add a large amount of black pepper.
  3. When the spaghetti is ready, flip them into a colander, then put them in a plate, adding a little water in which they were cooked.
  4. Gradually add 100 g of cheese, stirring constantly, so that it melts. Garnish with the remaining cheese and serve.
